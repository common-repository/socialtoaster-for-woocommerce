Integrate single sign-on and points with your SocialToaster campaign.

== Description ==

[SocialToaster](https://www.socialtoaster.com) is an artificial intelligence-powered content marketing platform that helps brands, businesses, and other organizations distribute content directly to their biggest fans, empowering them to engage with and share it with their family, friends, and others in their network.

The SocialToaster for WooCommerce plugin allows WooCommerce-enabled WordPress sites to integrate the capabilities of SocialToaster directly into their check-out process.

With the plugin installed, your store offers an easy way for customers to join your SocialToaster program, seamlessly integrating into your site with a single sign-on feature. When paired with the WooCommerce Points and Rewards plugin, the SocialToaster for WooCommerce plugin also allows you to reward your customers with points redeemable towards great prizes.

To learn more about the SocialToaster content marketing platform and how it can expedite your content marketing execution, please visit our [website](https://www.socialtoaster.com).

For help on integrating the SocialToaster for WooCommerce plugin, please email [support@socialtoaster.com](mailto:support@socialtoaster.com).

A new profile is automatically created for each new program user within your SocialToaster database.

= Single Sign-on Capabilities =

Once your user has signed up and created a profile within your program, the SocialToaster for WooCommerce plugin will allow your user to gain access to their SocialToaster account directly from your site via a single login.

Once logged in to your WooCommerce site, your users will also be logged into your SocialToaster site when they navigate to it.

= Points Integration =

Your SocialToaster program allows your users to earn points with each action they take on your behalf. With the SocialToaster for WooCommerce plugin, your users can easily earn and track their points directly from your integrated website. Enabling this functionality also requires the [WooCommerce Points and Rewards plugin](https://woocommerce.com/products/woocommerce-points-and-rewards/).
